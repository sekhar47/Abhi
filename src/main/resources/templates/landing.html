Frequently asked questions

Can’t find the answer you’re looking for? Reach out to our customer support team.

How do I register as a new user?
To register as a new user, simply click on the 'Register' button on the login page and fill out the required information.
Can I update my skills after registering?
Yes, you can update your skills at any time by logging in to your account and accessing the 'Update Skills' section.
How can I contact the admin for support?
You can contact the admin by clicking on the 'Contact Us' page and filling out the contact form with your query.
What kind of reports can the admin generate?
The admin can generate reports based on specific requirements such as skill assessment, user activity, and skill matrix analysis.
Can I view my skills after adding them?
Yes, you can view your skills by logging in to your account and accessing the 'View Skills' section
